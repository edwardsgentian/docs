# Authentication

Esooo uses Supabase Authentication to securely manage user accounts and sessions.

## Sign Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Click "Sign Up" in the header or navigate to the sign-in page and click the sign-up link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in the registration form:
    - **Name**: Your full name
    - **Email**: Your email address (must be unique)
    - **Phone**: Your phone number
    - **Password**: Create a secure password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click "Sign Up" to create your account. The system creates both a Supabase auth user and a player record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Verification">
    Check your email inbox for a confirmation email from Esooo. The email includes:
    - Esooo logo
    - Welcome message
    - Confirmation button/link
    - Styled to match the app's design
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Account">
    Click the confirmation link in the email to verify your account. You'll be redirected back to the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    After verification, you can optionally complete your player profile with:
    - Profile photo
    - Playing experience
    - Skill level
    - Preferred positions
    - Other soccer-related details
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign In

Simply enter your email and password to sign in. Your session will be maintained across browser sessions.

## Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## Profile Management

After signing in, you can:

- Update your profile information
- Add a profile photo
- Set your playing experience and skill level
- Specify preferred positions
- View your game history and statistics

## Security

- All passwords are securely hashed
- Sessions are managed securely through Supabase
- Email verification ensures account security
- Password reset requires email verification
