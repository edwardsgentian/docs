# Welcome to Esooo

<Note>
  **Esooo** (esooo.co) is a modern web application designed to help organize and manage soccer games, groups, and seasons. Whether you're a player looking to join games or an organizer managing a soccer group, Esooo provides all the tools you need.
</Note>

## What is Esooo?

Esooo is a comprehensive platform that enables:

- **Players** to discover and join soccer games and seasons in their area
- **Organizers** to create and manage games, seasons, and groups with powerful tools
- **Teams** to be automatically balanced based on player levels and positions
- **Payments** to be processed securely through Stripe integration
- **Communication** through automated email notifications and confirmations

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create an account with your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account before you can start using Esooo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Games">
    Explore available games and seasons on the homepage. Use filters to find games by city, day of week, or tags.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join a Game">
    Click on a game card to view full details including date, time, location, price, and current attendees. Click "Join Game" to proceed to secure Stripe checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Profile">
    Complete your profile with playing experience, skill level, preferred positions, and other details to help organizers create balanced teams.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform Capabilities

### For Players

- **Discover Games**: Browse games by location, date, and tags
- **Join Seasons**: Purchase season passes for multiple games
- **Manage Attendance**: Mark yourself as attending or not attending
- **Waitlist**: Join waitlists for full games and get notified when spots open
- **Profile**: Track your game history, statistics, and hours played
- **Notifications**: Receive email reminders and confirmations

### For Organizers

- **Group Management**: Create and manage soccer groups with custom branding
- **Game Creation**: Create individual games with flexible scheduling
- **Season Management**: Set up recurring game seasons with custom or weekly schedules
- **Team Planning**: Automatically balance teams with drag-and-drop customization
- **Member Management**: Track member levels, positions, and statistics
- **Budget Tracking**: Track revenue and expenses with OCR receipt scanning
- **Analytics**: View overview statistics including attendance, revenue, and member trends

## User Roles

### Players
Players can browse games, join games and seasons, manage their attendance, and view their game history.

### Organizers
Organizers have full control over their groups, including creating games and seasons, managing members, planning teams, and tracking finances.
