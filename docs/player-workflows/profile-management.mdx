# Profile Management

Your player profile helps organizers create balanced teams and allows you to track your soccer activity over time.

## Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click on your avatar or name in the header to access your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Profile">
    Your profile displays your information, statistics, and game history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Information

Your profile includes the following information:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic Information" icon="user">
    - **Name**: Your full name
    - **Email**: Your registered email address
    - **Phone**: Your contact number
    - **Profile Photo**: Your avatar image
  </Card>
  <Card title="Playing Details" icon="gamepad">
    - **Playing Experience**: Years of experience playing soccer
    - **Skill Level**: Your current skill level (1-5 scale)
    - **Preferred Positions**: Positions you prefer to play (Goalie, Defender, Mid-field, Forward)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Editing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Edit Mode">
    Click the "Edit Profile" button on your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Information">
    Modify any of the following fields:
    - Profile photo (upload new image)
    - Playing experience
    - Skill level
    - Preferred positions
    - Other soccer-related details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click "Save" to update your profile. Changes are saved immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your name, email, and phone number are managed through your account settings and cannot be changed from the profile page.
</Info>

## Skill Level

Your skill level helps organizers balance teams. Choose the level that best represents your current ability:

- **Level 1**: Beginner - New to soccer or returning after a long break
- **Level 2**: Recreational - Play occasionally, basic skills
- **Level 3**: Intermediate - Regular player with solid fundamentals
- **Level 4**: Advanced - Experienced player with strong technical skills
- **Level 5**: Expert - Highly skilled, competitive level

<Warning>
Be honest about your skill level. This helps organizers create fair and balanced teams for everyone's enjoyment.
</Warning>

## Statistics

Your profile tracks the following statistics:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Games Played" icon="gamepad">
    Total number of games you've participated in
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hours Played" icon="clock">
    Total hours spent playing soccer through Esooo
  </Card>
  <Card title="Seasons Joined" icon="calendar">
    Number of seasons you've purchased
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Statistics are automatically updated when you join games or seasons.

## Game History

Your profile displays a history of all games you've participated in:

- **Upcoming Games**: Games you're scheduled to attend
- **Past Games**: Games you've already played
- **Game Details**: Date, time, location, and group for each game

<Info>
You can click on any game in your history to view full details and see team compositions (if teams are visible).
</Info>

## Profile Visibility

Your profile information is visible to:

- **Organizers**: Can see your skill level, positions, and statistics to help with team planning
- **Other Players**: Can see basic information when viewing game attendee lists
- **Public**: Your name and avatar may appear on game pages

<Warning>
Your email and phone number are private and only visible to you.
</Warning>
