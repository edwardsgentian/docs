# Member Management

The Members tab in the organizer dashboard allows you to view and manage all players who have joined your group's games or seasons.

## Accessing Member Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organizer Dashboard">
    From your group page, click the "Organizer Dashboard" button. This opens the dashboard in a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Members Tab">
    In the sidebar navigation, click on the "Members" tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Member List">
    You'll see a table of all members who have participated in your group's activities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Member Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Member Name">
    Click on any member's name in the table to view their detailed profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Details">
    The member detail view shows:
    - Full profile information
    - Complete game history with your group
    - Statistics and participation trends
    - Contact information (if available)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filtering and Sorting

The members table supports:

- **Search**: Filter members by name
- **Sort**: Sort by name, level, games played, or last active date
- **View Options**: Adjust table display preferences

<Info>
Use the search and sort features to quickly find specific members or identify your most active participants.
</Info>

## Position Distribution

The members table shows each member's preferred positions, helping you:

- **Identify Goalies**: Find players who can play goalkeeper
- **Balance Positions**: Ensure teams have variety in positions
- **Plan Teams**: Use position data in the Team Planner

<Info>
Position information is used by the Team Planner to automatically balance teams. Members can have multiple preferred positions.
</Info>

## Member Activity

Track member participation over time:

- **Active Members**: Regularly join games
- **Inactive Members**: Haven't participated recently
- **New Members**: Recently joined your group

<Info>
Use the "Last Active" column to identify members who might need re-engagement or who are your most loyal participants.
</Info>

## Member Communication

While viewing member details, you can:

- **View Contact Info**: See email and phone (if member has shared)
- **Game History**: Review their participation in your group's games
- **Statistics**: Understand their playing patterns

<Warning>
Respect member privacy. Only use contact information for group-related communication.
</Warning>

## Using Member Data for Team Planning

Member information is automatically used in the Team Planner:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Planner">
    Navigate to the Planner tab in the organizer dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Balance">
    The planner uses member levels and positions to automatically create balanced teams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manual Adjustments">
    Review and adjust team assignments based on member data and your knowledge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
