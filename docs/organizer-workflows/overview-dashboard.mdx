# Overview Dashboard

The Overview dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your group's activity, revenue, attendance, and member trends at a glance.

## Accessing the Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organizer Dashboard">
    From your group page, click the "Organizer Dashboard" button. This opens the dashboard in a new tab at `esooo.co/[group-slug]/organizer`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Overview">
    The Overview tab is the first tab in the sidebar and loads by default when you open the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Financial Overview

### Revenue Metrics

The dashboard shows financial information:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Money In" icon="arrow-down">
    Total revenue from Stripe payments for games and seasons. Automatically calculated from all completed payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Money Out" icon="arrow-up">
    Total expenses recorded through the Budget tab. Includes OCR-scanned receipts and manual entries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Balance" icon="dollar-sign">
    Net balance calculated as Money In minus Money Out. Shows your group's current financial position.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recent Expenses" icon="receipt">
    Quick view of recent expenses from the budget tracking system.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
Revenue is automatically synced from Stripe payment records. You don't need to manually track income.
</Info>

## Dashboard Updates

The Overview dashboard updates in real-time:

- **Revenue**: Updates automatically when payments are processed
- **Attendance**: Updates when players join or leave games
- **Members**: Updates when new members join
- **Statistics**: Recalculates based on current data

<Info>
All metrics are calculated from your group's actual data, ensuring accuracy and up-to-date information.
</Info>

## Understanding Metrics

### Revenue Metrics
- **Money In**: All successful Stripe payments for games and seasons
- **Money Out**: All expenses you've recorded in the Budget tab
- **Balance**: Your net financial position

### Attendance Metrics
- **Total Games**: Count of all games created (past and future)
- **Total Attendance**: Sum of all player participations
- **Average Attendance**: Helps you understand typical game size

### Member Metrics
- **Total Members**: Unique players who have ever joined
- **Active Members**: Members with recent participation
- **Growth**: Indicates whether your group is expanding
