# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Esooo in just a few steps.

## For Players

### 1. Create an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Click "Sign Up" in the header and enter your name, email, phone number, and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email for a confirmation link. Click the link to verify your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Optionally complete your profile with playing experience, skill level, and preferred positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Browse Games

The homepage displays all upcoming games and seasons. You can:

- **Filter by City**: Use the city dropdown to find games in specific locations
- **Filter by Day**: Select days of the week to see games on those days
- **Filter by Tags**: Filter games by tags like "Beginner Friendly", "Competitive", etc.
- **View Groups**: Click on group names to see all games and seasons for that group

### 3. Join a Game

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Game Details">
    Click on any game card to see full details including:
    - Date and time
    - Location with map integration
    - Price per spot
    - Current attendees (up to 24 avatars visible)
    - Available spots vs total capacity
    - Game duration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Availability">
    Ensure the game has available spots. If full, you can join the waitlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase Spot">
    Click "Join Game" to proceed to Stripe checkout. Payment is processed securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    After payment, you'll receive a confirmation email and be added to the attendee list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Attendance">
    From the game page, you can toggle your attendance status (attending/not attending).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Organizers

### 1. Create a Group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Create Group">
    Click "Create Group" from the homepage or groups page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details">
    Enter group name, description, location, and upload photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Group">
    Your group is now created and you can start adding games and seasons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Create Your First Game

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Organizer Dashboard">
    From your group page, click "Organizer Dashboard" button (visible only to group creators). The dashboard opens in a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Games Tab">
    In the organizer dashboard sidebar, click on "Games" (third tab, with volleyball icon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click +Game">
    Click the "+Game" button in the header to start the game creation wizard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Game Creation Wizard">
    The wizard guides you through:
    - Basic info (name, description, location)
    - Schedule (date, time, duration)
    - Pricing (price per spot)
    - Capacity (total tickets, available tickets)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Game">
    Review your game details and click "Create Game" to save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 3. Plan Teams

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Planner Tab">
    In the organizer dashboard, navigate to the Planner tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Number of Teams">
    Enter how many teams you want (2-10).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Teams">
    Click "Generate Teams" to automatically balance teams based on player levels and positions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Drag and drop players between teams to adjust the balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save Teams" to save your team configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Concepts

### Games
Individual soccer matches with specific dates, times, and locations. Players can purchase spots to join.

### Seasons
Collections of games with recurring schedules. Players purchase a season pass to access all games in the season.

### Groups
Organizations that host games and seasons. Each group has an organizer who manages all activities.

### Teams
Balanced team configurations for games, automatically generated based on player levels and positions.
